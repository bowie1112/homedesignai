# Home Design AI

Home Design AI is an online AI home design tool for turning room photos, sketches, and layout ideas into visual design concepts.

Use it to explore interior design directions, virtual staging ideas, home exterior concepts, garden design, and floor plan visualization directly in your browser.

Online experience: [AI home design tool](https://homedesignai.co/)

## What It Helps With

- AI interior design from a room photo or text brief
- AI floor plan generation and visualization
- Virtual staging for real estate and renovation planning
- Home exterior, garden, and landscape design ideas
- Sketch-to-image concepts for early design exploration
